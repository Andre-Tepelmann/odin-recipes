Selfmade Chicken Döner

Selfmade Chicken Döner recipe like in Berlin City

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 table spoon Paprika Powder / Salt and Pepper
  • 1 Paprika
  • 4 Tomatoes
  • 1 Cucumber
  • Some Oil
  • An pinch Sugar and Lemon juice
  • Some Dill (Fresh or dry)
  • 1/4 parsley, sliced
  • 1 toe of Garlic
  • 1 Chili
  • 1 Cup of Yoghurt
  • 1/2 Flatbread

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken breast into cubes, sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of paprika powder, season with salt and pepper and fry in a pan. Dice the bell bell pepper, place in a separate pan and fry as well.
  2. Remove the seeds from the tomatoes and cut them into strips. Add the tomato and bell pepper strips to the chicken and keep warm (be careful to keep the tomatoes crisp, only warm them up slightly inside!).
  3. Dice the cucumber. Mix with some oil, salt, pepper, sugar, some lemon juice, dill and parsley.
  4. Press the garlic, chop the chili very finely. Add both to the yogurt. Season with salt, pepper and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  5. Cut the pita bread into quarters and bake in the oven. Fill the pita bread with the dip, cucumber salad and chicken mixture.
